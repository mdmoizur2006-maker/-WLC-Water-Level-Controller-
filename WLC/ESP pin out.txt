ESP32 pin connection
HC-SR04 Pin	ESP32 Pin

VCC	5V
GND	GND
TRIG	GPIO 5
ECHO	GPIO 18 ⚠

IMPORTANT

HC-SR04 ECHO gives 5V output.
ESP32 GPIO supports only 3.3V.

Use voltage divider:
ECHO ---- 1kΩ ---- GPIO18
            |
           2kΩ
            |
           GND


OLED Pin	ESP32 Pin

VCC	3.3V
GND	GND
SCL	GPIO 22
SDA	GPIO 21


Relay Pin	ESP32 Pin

IN	GPIO 26
VCC	5V
GND	GND


ESP32 ↔ Bluetooth Status LED

LED Part	ESP32 Pin
LED +	GPIO 2 through 220Ω resistor
LED -	GND


Water Sensor Pin	ESP32 Pin

One Wire	GPIO 27
Other Wire	GND
 
for more sensitive use BC547 transistor
